ABOUT SENTIRE

It is not a rare sight to find traditional wallpaper and flowery prints on resort wear today. A classical concept that Sentire believes can be further developed by illustrating not only a pattern, but also a statement or an unforeseen "twist". Sentire conveys a lifestyle, the garments can be worn all year and on different occasions, not only to the beach or when on holiday.
